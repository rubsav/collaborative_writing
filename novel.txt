He didn't know why they were there... but they were. They stared back, a deep black gaze. A cacophonous whir and strange green lights filled the room. Then, from the darkness, he heard someone yell "SPRIIIIIING BREEEEEEAAAAAAK!!!!".
The effect was instantaneous. The crowd reveled in decadent ecstasy as they partied through the night. The music reverberated through his bones and his reality was shifted.
